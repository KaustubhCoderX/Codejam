Chandrayaan-3 is India's third lunar mission. It will land a lander and rover on the lunar south pole, a region that has never been explored before. The mission will search for water ice and study the geology of the lunar south pole. The mission is a major milestone for the Indian space program. It is the first Indian mission to attempt a soft landing on the lunar south pole, and it is the first Indian mission to carry a rover to the moon.
