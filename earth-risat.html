The Radar Imaging Satellite (RISAT) mission marked a significant advancement in Earth observation through radar technology. Launched on April 20, 2009, RISAT aimed to provide all-weather surveillance and reconnaissance capabilities, essential for various applications including agriculture, forestry, disaster management, and defense.
