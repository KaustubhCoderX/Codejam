ISRO

Indian space research organisation, the space agency operating under the Department of Space (DOS), is dedicated to advancing space technology to meet the nation's requirements. It actively engages in satellite missions, remote sensing, and fosters the dissemination of science education. With its main headquarters situated in Bengaluru, ISRO maintains centers in Thiruvananthapuram, Bengaluru, Sriharikota, Valiamala, Ahmedabad, and Hyderabad. The Chairman plays a pivotal role, overseeing all activities and providing leadership to the Space Commission, which formulates policies and supervises the effective implementation of the Indian Space Programme.
