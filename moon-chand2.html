Fun Fact

Chandrayaan 2 was India's second lunar mission aimed at landing a rover on the Moon's south pole. Named after Vikram Sarabhai, it included the Vikram lander and Pragyan rover. Though the soft landing failed, the orbiter found water molecules on the lunar surface, making it a significant and cost-effective mission.
